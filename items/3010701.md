# Nesting and Material Cost

## 90-Degree Rotation Geometry

The label is **49.16" wide × 9.38" tall** when installed. During production the label is **rotated 90 degrees on the roll** so that:

- **9.38" (label height) feeds ACROSS the 24" roll width** — enables 2-across nesting
- **49.16" (label width) feeds ALONG the roll length** — becomes the cut length per row

This is the same length-based, length-feeds-along-roll convention used by every other length-based cut vinyl item on this account (1205720, 3010704, 3010707).

# Production Process

## Process Steps

1. **File import/setup** — open the Elliott-supplied production-ready DWG, send to cutter. ~5 min mechanical. NOT billable labor (see `PRICING_RULES.md` §22).
2. **Cut** — Roland SG3-300 on 24" Cardinal Red stock. 2 labels across (2 × 9.38" = 18.76" across 24" roll, 5.24" clearance). Single continuous cut path of the "ELLIOTT" wordmark, repeated 2-up per row.
3. **Weed** — remove excess vinyl around the 7 block characters (E ×3, L ×2, I ×1, O ×1, T ×2 — wait, "ELLIOTT" = E-L-L-I-O-T-T, so E ×1, L ×2, I ×1, O ×1, T ×2). Open counters on E and O make weeding efficient per character; the 49.16" weed span across the row of 2 labels is the dominant time factor.
4. **Mask** — apply TransferRite 582U transfer tape over weeded characters. 24" tape matches 24" vinyl exactly.
5. **Inspect** — verify cut accuracy, no lifted edges, character legibility across the 49.16" width.
6. **Package** — flat, masked with transfer tape carrier, ready for hand-application on crane body/boom.

## Production Notes

- **Lamination passes:** 0 (cut vinyl, no print)
- **Cut runs:** 1
- **Equipment:** Roland SG3-300 (24" Cardinal Red stock; 28" max cut width — well within tolerance)
- **Weeding complexity:** Low–medium. 7 block characters at medium scale, open counters on E ×1 and O ×1. Geometrically similar to the LRG sibling (3010704), but at smaller scale; comparable to the small-format model designations (1205720 "E190", 1186310 "E160") in character count and shape complexity. The 49.16" weed/mask span is wider than the small-format cluster (~33.5" wide) but narrower than the LRG (~70.8" wide) — fractional width-handling premium of ~0.5% above proportional captures this.
- **Cut feed length:** 55.16" per row of 2 labels. On the 50yd roll (1800" usable), 32 complete rows × 2 labels = 64 labels per roll.
