# Production Process

## Process Steps

1. **File import/setup** — open the Elliott-supplied production-ready DWG, send to cutter. ~5 min mechanical. NOT billable labor (see `PRICING_RULES.md` §22).
2. **Cut** — Roland SG3-300 on 24" Olympic Blue stock. 2 labels across, cut in rows. Estimated: 5-8 min per production run.
3. **Weed** — remove excess vinyl around "D115" block letters. 4 large characters, no fine detail, open geometry. Estimated: 10-15 min per unit at small quantities; 5-8 min/unit at production volume.
4. **Mask** — apply TransferRite 582U transfer tape over weeded characters. Squeegee for adhesion. Estimated: 3-5 min per label.
5. **Inspect** — verify cut accuracy, confirm no lifted edges, check character legibility.
6. **Package** — flat, masked with transfer tape carrier. Label is delivered ready for hand-application on equipment.
