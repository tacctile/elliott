## Process Steps (~25 minutes total job time for the entire 20-label program)

5. **Inspect** — print quality, laminate adhesion, kiss-cut depth, text legibility (small format — readability check is critical).
6. **Package** — flat, on liner, ready for hand application on the control panel.

Steps 2-6 total ~20 minutes for the entire 20-label job. Combined with the 5-minute file import, total job time is ~25 minutes.
