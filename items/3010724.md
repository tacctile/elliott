# Production Process

## Process Steps

1. **File import/setup** — open the Elliott-supplied production-ready DWG, send to cutter. ~5 min mechanical. NOT billable labor (Core Rule 7).
2. **Cut** — Roland SG3-300 on 24" White stock. 3 labels across (3 × 8" = 24" across the 24" roll, zero clearance per the Round 0 layout assumption). Single continuous cut path of the "G50" character set, repeated 3-up per row.
3. **Weed** — remove excess vinyl around the 3 alphanumeric characters (G, 5, 0). Low complexity, identical geometry to 3010722.
4. **Mask** — apply TransferRite 582U transfer tape over weeded characters. 24" tape matches 24" vinyl exactly.
5. **Inspect** — verify cut accuracy, no lifted edges, character legibility across the 21" width.
6. **Package** — flat, masked with transfer tape carrier, ready for hand-application on crane body/boom.

## Production Notes

- **Lamination passes:** 0 (cut vinyl, no print)
- **Cut runs:** 1
- **Equipment:** Roland SG3-300 (24" White stock; 28" max cut width)
- **Weeding complexity:** Low — identical to 3010722 (color-only difference).
- **Cut feed length:** 21" per row of 3 labels (as filed, no spacing allocation).
