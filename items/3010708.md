# Production Process

## Process Steps

1. **File import/setup** — open the Elliott-supplied production-ready DWG, send to cutter. ~5 min mechanical. NOT billable labor (see `PRICING_RULES.md` §22).
2. **Cut** — Roland SG3-300 on 24" Black stock. 5 labels across (5 × 4" = 20" across 24" roll, 4" clearance). Single continuous cut path for the "ElliottEquip.com" wordmark, repeated 5-up across the roll.
3. **Weed** — remove excess vinyl around the 16 mixed-case characters. Weeding considerations: 4 enclosed counters (e ×2, o ×1, p ×1), 2 isolated dots (period between "ElliottEquip" and "com", dot over lowercase "i"), tight descender channel on "q" adjacent to "u" stem. Each label requires careful weeding despite the small overall area.
4. **Mask** — apply TransferRite 582U transfer tape over weeded characters. 24" tape matches 24" vinyl exactly.
5. **Inspect** — verify cut accuracy, no lifted edges, character legibility across the 34.887" length, all enclosed counters cleanly removed, dots intact.
6. **Package** — flat, masked with transfer tape carrier, ready for hand-application.

## Production Notes

- **Lamination passes:** 0 (cut vinyl, no print)
- **Cut runs:** 1
- **Equipment:** Roland SG3-300 (24" Black stock; 28" max cut width)
- **Weeding complexity:** Elevated relative to Band A model-designation items. 16 mixed-case characters at small scale, 4 enclosed counters, 2 isolated dots, tight q-u channel. Per-label weeding time is comparable to a Band A "E190" weeding pass despite the smaller sq ft, because the character count and enclosed-counter count are higher.
- **Cut feed length:** 40.887" per row of 5 labels. On the 50yd roll (1800" usable), 44 complete rows × 5 labels = 220 labels per roll.

# Pricing Derivation

**Methodology:** Color-parity pricing with the Band C founding data point (P/N 3010707, Cardinal Red). Identical dimensions, identical process, identical tier table. Color is the only variable; price does not differ by color. Standard industry practice for multi-color variants of identical geometry.

**Benchmark item:** P/N 3010707 (Cardinal Red) — the canonical pricing anchor for Band C. The 4-wave AI validation record and final industry validation are documented in full in `items/3010707.md`. P/N 3010707 carries the worst-case material cost ($3.79/label) and is the basis for all margin reasoning in this band.

**Why identical pricing across colors:** All three P/Ns (3010707 Cardinal Red, 3010708 Black, 3010709 White) cover the same drawing (D/3010707), the same dimensions (34.887" × 4" = 0.969 sq ft), the same cut/weed/mask process, the same weeding complexity (16 mixed-case characters, 4 enclosed counters, 2 dots, tight q-u channel), and the same nesting layout (5-up on 24" × 50yd roll). Material cost varies by color, but the labor profile, the nesting profile, and the buyer's $/sq ft mental model do not. Differential pricing by color on identical geometry would create a procurement-side problem and would not survive the Wave 3 buyer-simulation test (Sean expects identical prices for color-only differences on the same drawing).
