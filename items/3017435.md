# Nesting and Material Cost

### On 24" Roll
- 2 labels per row (8.38" + 8.38" = 16.76", fits 24" width)
- Linear footage per row: ~43.91" (~1.22 yards)
- Vinyl cost per label: ~$8.00 (1.22 yards × $13.116/yard ÷ 2)
- Tape: ~$0.46
- **Total: ~$8.46/label**

### On 48" Roll (dramatically better yield)
- 5 labels across (8.38" × 5 = 41.9", fits 48" width)
- Linear footage per row: ~43.91" (~1.22 yards)
- Vinyl cost per label: ~$6.28 (1.22 yards × $25.744/yard ÷ 5)
- Tape: ~$0.46
- **Total: ~$6.74/label**

### Recommendation
48" roll for any order of qty 10+. The per-label savings ($1.72) justify the larger roll purchase. 24" roll for small or one-off orders where stocking 48" isn't warranted.
