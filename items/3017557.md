# Pricing Derivation

- **Methodology:** Material-proportional scaling from the Orajet printed/laminated singles band. This is the FOUNDING item for the Convex High Bond + Polycarbonate material family — no prior data point exists in this family. The price was derived through internal methodology, not external market benchmarking.

- **Derivation steps:**
  1. Interpolated the Orajet singles band $/sq ft at 1.102 sq ft (~$15.48/sq ft at qty 20)
  2. Calculated the material cost ratio: Convex+PC combined $/sq ft ÷ Orajet+polyester combined $/sq ft
     - Variant B: $3.5081 ÷ $1.9489 = **1.800×**
     - Variant A: $3.0576 ÷ $1.9489 = **1.569×**
  3. Scaled: $15.48 × ratio = target $/sq ft, multiplied by 1.102 sq ft area, snapped to $0.25 increments (§30)
     - Variant B: $15.48 × 1.800 = $27.86 → $27.90/sq ft at price of **$30.75**
     - Variant A: $15.48 × 1.569 = $24.29 → $24.27/sq ft at price of **$26.75**
  4. Built tiers using the standard account tier ratios (1-9 at ~1.50×, 10-19 at ~1.20×, etc.)
  5. Verified 87% margin at qty 20 — identical to every Orajet printed/laminated item

- **Comparable Items:**
  - **1230820** (1.296 sq ft, Orajet/polyester, $20.00 at qty 20, $15.43/sq ft, FA Accepted) — Root benchmark. Closest by size. Same process. The $/sq ft ratio between 3017557 Variant B and 1230820 equals the material cost ratio (1.80×), confirming proportional scaling.
  - **1278980** (0.609 sq ft, Orajet/polyester, $9.50 at qty 20, $15.60/sq ft) — Nearest validated singles-band data point by process equivalence.

- **Validation:**
  - No multi-round AI validation was run on the final locked price — pricing was locked by Nick.
  - Extensive external market research (3 rounds of Gemini deep research, 6 models per round) confirmed the market band for vinyl-base + polycarbonate-overlaminate overlays at qty 10–25 is $40–$70/sq ft. The recommended price sits ~30% below the market floor, consistent with the ~25–30% relationship concession on the Orajet band.
  - A full Claude Code methodology audit (Session AD) independently derived $30.75 by applying the account's own pricing rules, band structure, and margin targets to the new material family.
  - Prior external validation waves (dozens of AI model rounds) produced prices of $44–$57 at qty 20. These were rejected because every external model benchmarked against the rigid polycarbonate industrial nameplate/overlay market — the wrong reference class. P/N 3017557 is a printed vinyl label with a polycarbonate overlaminate, produced on the same equipment with the same process as every Orajet item on the account.

- **Override:** Strategic Anchor — this price establishes the founding $/sq ft band for the entire Convex/polycarbonate material family. Every future item in this family scales from this data point.

- **Profile Validation:** Establishes the Convex High Bond + Polycarbonate Pricing Profile. Variant B at $27.90/sq ft, Variant A at $24.27/sq ft at qty 20.
