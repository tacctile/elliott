## Process Steps

1. **File prep** — import 1186310 cut file, verify dimensions, set up tool path. Estimated: 15-20 min (one-time setup).
2. **Cut** — Roland SG3-300 on 24" Cardinal Red stock. 2 labels across, cut in rows.
