# Production Process

## Process Steps

1. **File import/setup** — open the Elliott-supplied production-ready DWG, send to cutter. ~5 min mechanical. NOT billable labor (see `PRICING_RULES.md` §22).
2. **Cut** — Roland SG3-300 on 24" Cardinal Red stock. 2 labels across, cut in rows.
3. **Weed** — remove excess vinyl around "E160" block letters. 4 large characters, no fine interior detail, open geometry.
4. **Mask** — apply TransferRite 582U transfer tape over weeded characters.
5. **Inspect** — verify cut accuracy, no lifted edges, character legibility.
6. **Package** — flat, masked with transfer tape carrier, ready for hand-application.
